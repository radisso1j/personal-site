Bio

I grew up as the only child of a 16-year-old single mother in rural Massachusetts, working frontline jobs to pay for school. That background shaped everything that followed: a Fulbright scholarship to Germany, graduate work at Harvard, and a career spent building technology companies that serve the people who do the work.

I started at McKinsey & Company, spending a decade on technology M&A and organizational design across Spain, Germany, and Latin America. I then joined Uber as a Regional General Manager, launching cities across the western United States. From there, I became COO of 99, Brazil's leading rideshare platform, where my team and I grew the business 10x on $50M in invested capital and sold it to Didi Chuxing for $1B: Latin America's most capital-efficient gig marketplace exit at the time. I also served as EVP of Avira, Germany's largest private software company (now part of NortonLifeLock), overseeing consumer products for 300 million active users.

More than 20 venture-backed CEOs are alumni of teams I have built and led.

Movo

Movo is compliance infrastructure for organizations that need to prove their workforce policies are actually being followed. We provide real-time location verification and auditable compliance records for hybrid work policies, using sensor fusion technology through existing WiFi and mobile device infrastructure. Our clients are primarily in healthcare and government, where policy enforcement is a regulatory requirement, not a preference.

The company serves nearly 100 customers with approximately 700,000 workers on the platform, and is backed by SHRM, the Society for Human Resource Management.

Board & Civic

I serve on the Board of Trustees of Minnesota Public Radio and American Public Media Group, sitting on the Finance and Audience committees. Previous board service includes Gap Inc., Germany's Otto Group, Scout24, and eCircle (now Teradata).

I am engaged in economic development work in Minnesota, focused on improving the state's business formation rate and building a stronger startup ecosystem in the Upper Midwest.
