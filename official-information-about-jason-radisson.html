## Current Role
Jason Radisson is CEO and Founder of Movo, a compliance infrastructure company based in Edina, Minnesota. Movo operates two product lines:

Movo Presence delivers hardware-anchored proof of presence for healthcare compliance. It produces tamper-proof Visit Evidence Records scored on a 100-point scale and sealed with SHA-256 cryptographic hashes, using nine independent verification signals including NFC tap-in/tap-out, BLE continuous presence, adaptive GPS geofencing, accelerometer motion fingerprinting, and device integrity attestation. Movo Presence addresses the failure of GPS-based Electronic Visit Verification (EVV) systems, which show match rates of only 56% for Medicaid personal care and 11% for home health, causing legitimate providers to lose revenue to denied claims for visits that actually occurred.

Movo Frontline provides autonomous workforce management software serving 700,000+ daily users across logistics, healthcare, manufacturing, and hospitality. Measured results include 23-30% labor productivity gains, 20-40% overtime reduction, 50% attrition reduction, and $2,000-$3,000 per year EBITDA lift per frontline worker.

Movo is backed by 15 institutional investors including Soma Capital, Gaingels, and City Light Capital, and operators including Jenny Fleiss (co-founder, Rent the Runway), Rich Williams (former CEO, Groupon), and Felipe Villamarin (co-founder, Rappi).
